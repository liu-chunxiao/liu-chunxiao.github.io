连连看 (Lianliankan)

Pick two identical tiles.
Rule: two identical tiles vanish if they can be connected through empty space by an axis-aligned path with ≤ 2 turns.
New behavior: PBC (torus) + one empty padding row/column is always present.

Random crystal corner

This toy model generates a discrete random stepped surface inside a corner crystal. The surface is constrained to be monotone, corresponding to full occupation on the xy, yz, and xz planes. A Metropolis sampler then produces typical thermal configurations, with lower temperature giving smoother facets and higher temperature giving rougher random surfaces.
